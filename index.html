About Me

I am a legal researcher and academic focused on criminal law, with special emphasis on smuggling offenses in Kosovo. My research examines the judicial handling of smuggling crimes in the Basic Court of Mitrovica from 2008-2011.

This work forms part of my academic studies under the mentorship of Prof. Dr. Azem Hajdari at the University of Prishtina, where I completed my research in 2018.

My academic interests include criminal law, international trade regulations, customs law, and judicial practice in Kosovo's developing legal system.

Featured Research

Smuggling as a Criminal Offense with Special Focus on the Territory of the Basic Court of Mitrovica 2008-2011

Mentor: Prof. Dr. Azem Hajdari Completed: 2018 Prishtina, Kosovo

This comprehensive study examines the criminal offense of smuggling goods in Kosovo, with a particular focus on cases processed by the Basic Court of Mitrovica between 2008-2011. The research analyzes legal, criminological, and practical aspects of smuggling offenses in the context of Kosovo's developing legal system.

Key Research Areas:

  • Legal definition of smuggling under Kosovo's Criminal Code
  • Types of customs offenses according to Kosovo's Customs and Excise Code
  • Manifestations of smuggling and its various forms
  • Perpetrators of smuggling offenses and criminal liability
  • Comparative analysis with smuggling legislation in Slovenia, Albania, Montenegro, Serbia, and Croatia
  • Criminological aspects including phenomenology and etiology of smuggling offenses
  • Preventive and repressive measures for combating smuggling crimes
